About Us

Vision

Welcome to presshut News! My name is Abdullah Azhar and I am the owner and editor-in-chief of this website. I have always been fascinated by the world of journalism and the role it plays in informing and shaping public opinion. As a young boy, I would spend hours devouring the news, trying to understand the events and issues of the day. As I grew older, my passion for journalism only deepened, and I knew that I wanted to make it my career. After earning my degree in journalism, I began my career as a reporter for a local newspaper. Over the years, I have worked for a number of different news organizations, covering a wide range of topics and issues. In 2022, I decided to take the leap and launch my own news website. I wanted to create a platform that would provide reliable, accurate, and unbiased news to readers around the world. Here at presshut News, we are dedicated to delivering the latest news and analysis on a wide range of topics, including politics, business, sports, entertainment, and more. Our team of dedicated journalists works around the clock to bring you the most important and interesting stories from around the world. We believe that accurate and fair journalism is more important now than ever before. In an age where fake news and propaganda are rampant, it is our mission to provide our readers with the truth, no matter how uncomfortable or inconvenient it may be. In addition to our daily news coverage, we also offer in-depth analysis and commentary on the issues that matter most to our readers. Our team of experienced writers and analysts brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every piece we publish, ensuring that our readers receive the most informed and thoughtful perspective on the stories that matter most. We hope that you will join us on this journey and become a regular reader of Abdullah Azhar's News. Thank you for visiting our website, and please don't hesitate to reach out to us with any comments, questions, or suggestions.



why is journalism important

Journalism plays a vital role in any healthy democracy. It helps to inform the public about important issues, hold those in power accountable, and give a voice to the marginalized and underserved. One of the main functions of journalism is to provide people with accurate and reliable information. In a world where misinformation and fake news are rampant, it is more important than ever to have a source that can be trusted to report the truth. Good journalism helps to cut through the noise and provide a clear and unbiased view of the facts. Journalism also serves as a check on those in power. Whether it's exposing corruption or holding politicians accountable for their actions, a free and independent press is essential in keeping those in positions of authority honest. Without journalism, there would be little to prevent abuse of power and corruption from going unchecked. In addition to informing and holding accountable, journalism also gives a voice to the voiceless. By telling the stories of those who may not otherwise be heard, journalism can bring attention to important issues and bring about needed change. In short, journalism is important because it helps to create an informed and engaged citizenry, ensures transparency and accountability in government, and gives a voice to the marginalized and underserved. Without a free and independent press, democracy itself is at risk.
